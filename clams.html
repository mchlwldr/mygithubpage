Clams

The clam is an incredible species. First evolving over 500 million years ago, the clam has proven to be a hardy organism. It has managed to outlive an untold number of species, including the dinosaurs. Now they are found across the globe and in a wide range of shapes in sizes. Some clams are even eaten. Clams are an important part of underwater ecosystems, so it makes sense to learn a bit about this ancient survivor
